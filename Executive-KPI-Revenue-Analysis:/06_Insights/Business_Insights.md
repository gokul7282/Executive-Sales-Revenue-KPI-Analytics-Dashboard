# 📊 Business Insights – Executive KPI & Sales Dashboard

## 📌 Executive Summary
- The dashboard provides a complete overview of sales, profit, customer, product, and regional performance.
- Total Revenue shows strong overall performance across the dataset period.
- Total Profit indicates steady business growth with stable margins.
- Sales performance is driven by a few key products, customers, and regions.

---

## 📈 Sales Analysis
- Sales performance peaks in the later months of the year (November–December).
- Revenue shows an increasing trend toward the end of the period.
- Early months contribute lower revenue compared to peak months.
- Sales are not evenly distributed across all months.

---

## 👥 Customer Analysis
- Customer distribution is balanced across major regions.
- A small group of customers contributes a large share of revenue.
- No single region dominates customer base significantly.
- Customer behavior shows consistent contribution across markets.

---

## 📦 Product Analysis
- Technology category is the highest revenue and profit generating category.
- A small number of products contribute majority of total revenue.
- Some product names were cleaned due to invalid or blank values.
- Product performance follows a Pareto distribution (80/20 rule).

---

## 🌍 Regional Analysis
- United States generated the highest profit among all countries.
- APAC region contributed the highest revenue.
- Revenue is concentrated in a few key countries and markets.
- Top states contribute major share of overall revenue.

---

## 🎯 Key Business Insights
- Revenue is driven by top-performing categories and products.
- APAC and US are the strongest performing regions.
- Business shows dependency on few high-value customers.
- Sales trend improves significantly in later months.
- Data cleaning was required to remove invalid product entries.
